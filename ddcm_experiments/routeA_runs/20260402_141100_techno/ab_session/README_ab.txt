Blind listening instructions
===========================

Goal: pick which sample sounds BETTER (quality). This is NOT "guess the original".
Both files are mono — A/B are just two labels, not stereo left/right.

1. Do NOT open ab_key.json until after you record your preference.
2. Listen to sample_A.wav and sample_B.wav (same volume, same headphones).
3. Edit scores_template.csv: preferred_sample = A | B | tie
4. confidence_1_to_5: 1=guess, 5=strong preference
5. After saving scores, open ab_key.json to see which label was reference vs matched.
